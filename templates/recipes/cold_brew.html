Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew

The closest you can get to Starbucks at home.


The Story:
At the beginning of the 2020 quarantine Starbucks was closed for months,
leaving me missing the taste of my favorite item:
The Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew

Desperate, and out of options, I decided I would try to make this recipe myself.


In the beginning, I was making cold brew on a regular basis.

This took a few attempts to get to my liking...
But after controlling the coffee used, temperature, and brew time, I found a flavor that I really enjoyed.

This cold brew will be the base for the vanilla sweet cream cold brew.
